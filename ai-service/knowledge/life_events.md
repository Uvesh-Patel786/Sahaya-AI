# Life Events Guidance (Public Information Summary)

## Marriage
- Obtain marriage registration from the local registrar where applicable.
- Update KYC documents if name or marital status changes.
- Review family insurance nominees and ration card entries.

## Childbirth
- Register birth and collect the birth certificate.
- Follow immunization schedules at public health centres.
- Explore eligible maternal and child nutrition schemes via MyScheme.

## Starting a business
- Consider Udyam registration for MSMEs.
- Explore Startup India recognition and state incubators.
- Never pay unofficial “facilitation fees” for licences.

## Retirement
- Check EPFO / NPS / pension status via official portals.
- Review senior citizen health and welfare schemes for your state.
