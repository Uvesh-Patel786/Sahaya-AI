# Sahayak AI — Seed Knowledge Base (official-style public information summaries)

## PM-KISAN
PM-KISAN provides income support to eligible landholding farmer families.
Installments are paid directly to bank accounts subject to eligibility and eKYC.
Official portal: https://pmkisan.gov.in/
Always verify exclusion criteria on the official website before applying.

## National Scholarship Portal
The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) hosts multiple central and state scholarships.
Students must ensure institute verification and correct income/caste certificates.
Official portal: https://scholarships.gov.in/

## Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY
PM-JAY provides health coverage for eligible families at empanelled hospitals.
Citizens should check eligibility on https://pmjay.gov.in/ and never share OTP with agents.

## Stand-Up India
Stand-Up India supports SC/ST and women entrepreneurs with bank loans for greenfield enterprises.
Portal: https://www.standupmitra.in/

## Scam safety
Government departments do not ask for OTP, ATM PIN, or gift cards over phone or WhatsApp.
Verify messages using official portals and apps such as MyGov, DigiLocker, and UMANG.

## Civic reporting
Local civic issues such as potholes, garbage, and streetlights are generally handled by the municipal corporation or urban local body.
Keep photos, location, and date when filing a complaint.

## Documents
Keep Aadhaar, PAN, address proof, and income certificates updated in DigiLocker when possible.
Renew passports and licences before expiry and track deadlines carefully.
